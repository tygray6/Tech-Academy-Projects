My Reviews and Insights

As a commuter

A couple months ago, I suffered the commuter's nightmare: my bike was stolen. Being a bikeophile really salvaged the situation- I used the Blackbuck. For twenty bucks, I got it up and running for commuting duties and it has been great.

Fortune smiles on hoarders sometimes- I've been sitting on my 29er commuting kit for a year and right before I was about to sell it, this emergency came up. Because the bike has no eyelets, I bought a pair of Axiom Fender Runners and I was good to go.

As a mountain bike

It is a great mountain bike- pretty, compliant and room for a variety of tires. As a SS bike, one can't ask for much more. I did end up bying a headst to slack out the headangle by a degree, which has slowed down the steering to a comfortable level.

It climbs really well, which was surprising because of the chainstays- I expected worse, but so far, it is a perfect SS. The only gripe I have at this juncture is that on tight twisties the back end is slow to whip around and requires some body english.
